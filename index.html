About the app

Built for everyday care and urgent moments

Mediconn aims to bridge the gap between patients and caregivers with one place for support, updates, and emergency response. The app can grow into a wider care network with features like medication reminders and health insights. The goal is to make it easier for patients to get help when they need it, and for caregivers to stay informed and coordinated without confusion.
